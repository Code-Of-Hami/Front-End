Nepal Humanitarian Association volunteers have carried out many social activities in India in the past 11 years.

  • Educational Institutions

     We contribute to the continuation of the education of “children” with our trained instructors in the quality and healthy education environments in the training centers.

     Due to financial inadequacy, early work, educational environment and inadequate trainer quality, many children cannot benefit from education opportunities in Nepal.

  • Water Wells

     We can explain "sanitation" as creating a healthy living space for all living things and protecting natural resources such as surface waters, groundwater and soil. In areas lacking sanitation, cholera, hepatitis, polio and many more continue to die.

     In Nepal, where sanitation and infrastructure quality is very low, we open the water wells and enable the people there to access clean water and thereby protect them from deadly diseases.

    • Food Aid

     The biggest cause of hunger worldwide is naturally shown as poverty and unemployment. Over 4 million people in Nepal live under the hunger limit.

     Protein needs must be met for the physical development of children. Millions of people cannot afford food consumption to meet these needs. We deliver basic food packages from our volunteers to poor people.
